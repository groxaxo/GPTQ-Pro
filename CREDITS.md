GPTQ-Pro is a focused fork of GPTQModel. Some projects below are credited for
historical or upstream influence even when their runtime backends are not
shipped in this single-backend fork.

- [Qubitium](https://x.com/qubitium) and the [ModelCloud](https://x.com/ModelCloudAI) team for maintaining and improving GPTQModel.
- **Elias Frantar**, **Saleh Ashkboos**, **Torsten Hoefler**, and **Dan Alistarh** for [GPTQ](https://github.com/IST-DASLab/gptq) and [Marlin](https://github.com/IST-DASLab/marlin). Marlin is an external reference baseline here, not a bundled backend.

- **FpgaMiner** for [GPTQ-Triton](https://github.com/fpgaminer/GPTQ-triton) and its influence on the wider GPTQ ecosystem.
- **Casper Hansen** for [AutoAWQ](https://github.com/casper-hansen/AutoAWQ). AWQ support is not bundled in this fork.
